My Story

After completing the Machine Learning (cs229) and Computer Vision (cs231) courses, I became interested in pursuing research in the field of machine learning and artificial intelligence.

I started reaching out to professors around the world to inquire about research collaboration opportunities and was able to secure several internships.

These internships allowed me to gain hands-on experience in various areas of computer vision, such as object detection, image restoration, and biometrics.

I also explored different AI project ideas in computer vision, which helped me to gain a deeper appreciation for the sophistication of these techniques and their potential applications.

As I continue to develop my skills in programming languages, data engineering, and models, I am excited to explore new frontiers in the field of AI and contribute to the development of innovative solutions that can benefit society.
